About Me

My name is Jesse Bartola and I am an 18 years old. I am an aspiring software engineer currently attending the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I grew up around computers, mainly because my father had a job in the field as a network administrator. Since then I have developed a passion for programming web and mobile applications. I possess an unsual yearning for learning and will spend however long it takes to perfect every skill in my aresenal of knowledge.

What am I all about?


Learning

I can't stress how imporant it is these days to have an ambition for education. I'm always willing to learn new things on my own time and will go out of my way to make sure I am up to date with the latest and most elegant technology.

Technology

From calculators to iPhones to computers and beyond, working with technology is and has always been one of my biggest fortes. I am always trying to find the simpleist and most efficient ways to tackle any problems that I may encounter.

Active Engagement

Learning is one thing, but you start to make real progress when you can apply what you've learned to hands-on activites. Because of this, I am always involved in a few projects of my own. Making projects with the material you have learned is a great way to show and further develop your skill set.

TxtAdvice (OutFitr)

Instant fashion advice

Ever second-think youself after putting on an outfit before you head out the door? Few people haven't, which is why I decided to become the lead platform developer of TxtAdvice. At any moment a professional stylist is ready to answer any question you might have about your current outfit. Send in a question or a picture and you'll have an answer in no time!

Want to try it out? Go ahead down to txtadvice.com and enter your phone number in the box!

  • Date: June 2015

Basic Chat

Portable chat messaging

Basic Chat is a small project making good use of Node.js and MongoDB. It allows the user to send instant messages to other users that are logged in. The site also tracks analytics for each user that can be viewed on their profile. Check out the project on Github! More features are coming soon.
